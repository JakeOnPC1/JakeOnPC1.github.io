🔥 Thermodynamics in Chemistry

Thermodynamics studies energy changes during chemical reactions. Key laws:

  • Zeroth law – Thermal equilibrium.
  • First law – Energy conservation: ΔU = Q – W.
  • Second law – Entropy (S) of an isolated system always increases.
  • Third law – Entropy approaches zero at absolute zero.

Enthalpy (H) – heat content at constant pressure.
Gibbs free energy (ΔG = ΔH – TΔS) predicts spontaneity: ΔG < 0 → spontaneous.

⚡ Exothermic reactions release heat (negative ΔH). Endothermic absorb heat.
