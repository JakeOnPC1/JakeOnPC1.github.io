⚛️ Periodic Table of Elements

118 elements · Atomic structure · Chemical families

The periodic table organizes all known chemical elements by increasing atomic number and recurring chemical properties. It is the foundation of modern chemistry.

📊 Key Groups

GroupNameProperties
1Alkali metalsVery reactive, soft, low density
2Alkaline earth metalsReactive, form alkaline oxides
17HalogensHighly reactive nonmetals
18Noble gasesInert, colorless, odorless

🧬 Atomic Structure & Trends

  • Atomic radius increases down a group, decreases across a period.
  • Electronegativity increases left to right, decreases top to bottom.
  • Ionization energy – energy to remove an electron; highest for noble gases.

The modern table (Mendeleev's legacy) predicts undiscovered elements and their properties. It is a must-know for any scientist.

💡 Did you know? Only 94 of 118 elements occur naturally on Earth.
