⚡ Electrochemistry

Links chemical reactions to electricity. Redox (reduction‑oxidation) reactions transfer electrons.

  • Galvanic cells – spontaneous reaction produces electricity (batteries).
  • Electrolytic cells – electricity drives non‑spontaneous reaction (electrolysis).
  • Standard reduction potentials – measure tendency to gain electrons.
  • Nernst equation – cell potential under non‑standard conditions.

Applications: batteries, fuel cells, corrosion prevention, electroplating.

🔋 Lithium‑ion batteries power most portable electronics.
