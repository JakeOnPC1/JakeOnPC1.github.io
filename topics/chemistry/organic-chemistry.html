🌿 Organic Chemistry

Study of carbon compounds. Carbon forms 4 bonds – chains, rings, functional groups.

  • Alkanes – single bonds (CₙH₂ₙ₊₂).
  • Alkenes – double bonds, reactive.
  • Alcohols – -OH group (ethanol).
  • Carboxylic acids – -COOH (acetic acid).
  • Polymers – plastics, proteins, DNA.

Reactions: substitution, addition, elimination. Organic chemistry is the basis of life and pharmaceuticals.

🧬 DNA is an organic polymer – deoxyribonucleic acid.
