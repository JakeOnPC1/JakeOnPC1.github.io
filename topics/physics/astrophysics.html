🔭 Astrophysics & Cosmology

Astrophysics applies physics to understand celestial objects: stars, galaxies, black holes, and the universe as a whole.

Key Topics

  • Stellar evolution – from protostar to white dwarf, neutron star, or black hole.
  • Black holes – regions where gravity prevents anything (even light) from escaping. Event horizon, singularity.
  • Dark matter & dark energy – 95% of the universe is invisible. Dark matter binds galaxies; dark energy accelerates expansion.
  • Cosmic microwave background – afterglow of the Big Bang.
🌌 The Milky Way contains over 100 billion stars and a supermassive black hole (Sagittarius A*).
