🔥 Thermodynamics (Physics)

Study of heat, work, temperature, and energy transfer.

  • Zeroth law – thermal equilibrium.
  • First law – ΔU = Q – W (energy conservation).
  • Second law – entropy increases; heat cannot flow from cold to hot spontaneously.
  • Third law – entropy zero at absolute zero.
  • Heat engines – convert heat to work (Carnot efficiency).

Concepts: internal energy, enthalpy, entropy, free energy. Thermodynamics governs everything from engines to life.

❄️ Absolute zero is -273.15°C – impossible to reach.
