☢️ Nuclear Physics

Study of atomic nuclei – protons and neutrons held by strong nuclear force.

  • Radioactivity – alpha, beta, gamma decay.
  • Half‑life – time for half of nuclei to decay.
  • Nuclear fission – splitting heavy nuclei (uranium) releases energy.
  • Nuclear fusion – combining light nuclei (hydrogen) powers stars.
  • Applications – nuclear power, medicine (PET scans, radiotherapy), carbon dating.

E = mc² explains the enormous energy from tiny mass changes in nuclear reactions.

☀️ The Sun fuses 600 million tons of hydrogen each second.
