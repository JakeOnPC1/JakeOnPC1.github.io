🌠 Einstein's Relativity

Two theories: Special (1905) and General (1915).

Special Relativity

  • Laws of physics are same for all inertial observers.
  • Speed of light (c) is constant in vacuum.
  • Consequences: time dilation, length contraction, E = mc².

General Relativity

Gravity is curvature of spacetime caused by mass. Predicts black holes, gravitational waves, and bending of light (gravitational lensing).

⏱️ GPS satellites must correct for both special and general relativistic effects – otherwise they'd drift kilometers each day.
