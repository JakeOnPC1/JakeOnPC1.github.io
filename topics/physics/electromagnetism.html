🧲 Electromagnetism

Unified theory of electricity and magnetism. Maxwell's equations describe electric and magnetic fields.

  • Coulomb's law – force between charges.
  • Electric fields – from charges.
  • Magnetic fields – from moving charges (currents).
  • Electromagnetic induction – changing magnetic field induces current (Faraday's law).
  • Electromagnetic waves – light, radio, X‑rays (travel at c).

Applications: motors, generators, transformers, wireless communication.

📡 Radio waves are electromagnetic – they travel at 300,000 km/s.
